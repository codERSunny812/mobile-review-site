New Vivo X-series phone spotted on TENAA, 3C certification sites: design, specs revealed
Vivo V2219A comes with an unspecified 5G processor.
HIGHLIGHTS
  • Vivo X-series phone with model number V2219A has been spotted on TENAA and 3C certification websites.
  • The phone has a 4,400mAh battery that supports 80W fast charging and an 8MP selfie camera.
  • Vivo X-series phone has a centred punch-hole cutout display and a dual rear camera setup.
Vivo recently released its flagship Vivo X80-series, including a vanilla Vivo X80 and Vivo X80 Pro. It seems like the company is gearing up to launch another X-series smartphone. And now, an unknown Vivo X-series smartphone has surfaced online on TENNA and 3C certification websites, revealing its design and key specifications. The phone has a Vivo V2219A model number and an unspecified 5G SoC. Let’s take a look at what the Vivo X-series phone has in stores for us.
Vivo X-series phone TENAA listing
The TENAA listing of the Vivo V2219A(via) reveals the complete design of the smartphone. It has a thin bezel display with a centred punch-hole cutout at the front. The phone has a volume rocker and a power button on the left. The Vivo V2219A has a dual-camera setup housed in a square camera island alongside an LED flash on the back. The specs imply that the phone may be a “Lite” version in the series.
Vivo V2219A specifications

Vivo V2219A specs

  • 6.67-inch Full-HD+ AMOLED panel
  • Unspecified 5G chipset
  • Up to 12GB RAM, 512GB storage
  • 4,400mAh battery, 80W charging
  • 50MP + 2MP dual rear camera
  • 8MP selfie camera
  • Android 12-based OriginOS
The Vivo V2219A TENAA and 3C certifications reveal that the phone will come with a 6.67-inch Full-HD+ AMOLED panel. Under the hood, the device draws power from an unspecified 5G chipset that is clocked at 2.2GHz paired with 6GB/8GB/12GB RAM and 128GB/256GB/512GB storage. A 4,400mAh battery that supports 80W fast charging will power the package.
Moving to the optics, the Vivo V2219A comes with a dual rear camera setup, including a 50MP primary camera and a 2MP secondary shooter. An 8MP camera on the front will take care of selfies and video calling needs. Vivo V2219A is likely to boot Android 12-based OriginOS out of the box.
