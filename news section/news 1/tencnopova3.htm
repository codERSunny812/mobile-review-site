Tecno Pova 3 launching in India soon, Amazon teaser reveals: price, specifications
Tecno Pova 3 is expected to debut under Rs 15,000 price tag in India.
HIGHLIGHTS
  • Tecno Pova 3 will soon launch in India, suggests a teaser on Amazon.
  • The phone features a 50MP triple rear camera, up to 11GB RAM, and a side-mounted fingerprint scanner.
  • Tecno Pova 3 will be available in three colour options: Black, Blue, and Silver.
Tecno Pova 3 will soon launch in India, a new teaser on Amazon suggests. The poster also reveals key Tecno Pova 3 specifications, colour options, and design. The handset was recently launched in the Philippine market and is now making its way to the Indian smartphone market. The Indian unit of the Tecno Pova 3 was recently spotted on the BIS certification website with model number LF7.
Tecno Pova 3 has a boxy design with slightly curved edges. It has a side-mounted fingerprint reader and a triple rear camera. Let’s take a look at what the Tecno Pova 3 has in store for us.
Tecno Pova 3 Amazon listing has revealed its key specification, colour options, and design. The phone comes in three colour options: Black, Blue, and Silver. The launch date and price of the Tecno Pova 3 are still under the wraps, but we expect to learn more about it in the upcoming days. It was launched in the Philippines market at a price of PHP 8,999 (Approx. Rs 13,000). We can expect it to retail at around Rs 15,000 in India.
Tecno Pova 3 specifications

Tecno Pova 3 specs
  • 6.9-inch Full HD+ LCD 90Hz display
  • MediaTek Helio G88 SoC
  • 7,000mAh battery, 33W charging
  • 6GB + 5GB RAM, 128GB storage
  • 50MP triple rear camera
  • 8MP selfie camera
  • Android 11-based HiOS

Tenco Pova 3 has a 6.9-inch Full HD+ LCD panel that supports a 90Hz refresh rate and 2460×1080 pixel resolution. Under the hood, it draws power from a MediaTek Helio G88 processor. The phone packs a massive 7,000mAh battery that supports 33W fast charging. Tecno Poca 3 comes in 64GB/128GB storage and 4GB/6GB RAM, expandable up to 11GB via virtual RAM expansion. It’s unclear whether the Indian unit of Teno Pova 3 will support a microSD card.
Moving to optics, the Tenco Pova 3 houses a triple rear camera setup that features a 50MP primary camera. An 8MP camera at the front takes care of selfies and video calling needs. Tenco Pova 3 runs on Android 11-based HiOS out of the box and has a side-mounted fingerprint scanner for security.
