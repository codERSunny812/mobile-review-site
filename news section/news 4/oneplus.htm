New OnePlus 10 series phone with Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 processor tipped to soon launch
Here's what we know about the OnePlus 10 so far.
HIGHLIGHTS
  • OnePlus 10 with codename Ovaltine is tipped to be in the works.
  • The phone is tipped to pack a Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 SoC.
  • OnePlus 10 is said to launch later this year.
Oneplus 10 has been a longly pending and awaited handset ever since the OnePlus 10 Pro’s debut. While the phone has been in the headlines multiple times, the company has not revealed any plans to launch the vanilla OnePlus 10. OnePlus did launch a 10R, skipping the vanilla OnePlus 10, and is reportedly gearing up to launch a OnePlus 10 Ultra soon. Word on the streets is that the OnePlus 10T will be the last flagship phone from the OnePlus 10-series this year.
Now, tipster Yogesh Brar claims that a OnePlus phone with codename Ovaltine with Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 SoC is in the works and could likely be the OnePlus 10. Let’s have a look.
The OnePlus 10 is expected to launch later this year, but no specific date has been tipped yet. According to the tipster, the OnePlus 10 with codename Ovaltine will soon make its debut, carrying a Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 processor. The company has not confirmed the existence of the OnePlus 10 or any plans to launch the smartphone, so we suggest you take this leak with a pinch of salt.
OnePlus 10 specifications (expected)

OnePlus 10 specs
  • 6.7-inch FHD+ AMOLED 120Hz display
  • Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 SoC
  • 4,800mAh battery, 150W charging
  • Up to 12GB RAM, 256GB storage
  • Android 12-based Oxygen 12
  • 50MP+16MP+2MP triple rear camera

OnePlus 10-series houses Hasselblad cameras, and we can expect the same on the OnePlus 10. The phone is said to come with a triple rear camera setup, including a 50MP primary, a 16MP ultra-wide, and a 2MP macro camera. We expect things to be clearer soon after the company launches its next OnePlus 10-series smartphone.
